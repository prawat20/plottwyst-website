How to Play

Plottwyst is a cooperative murder mystery game for Discord. 2–5 detectives work together to identify a murderer across 4 rounds of evidence — but every case contains a deliberate misdirection designed to fool you.

⚠️ The Plottwyst: Every case has a built-in misdirection woven into the evidence from clue one. The opening evidence may point confidently in the wrong direction. Trust the clues — but question what they're really telling you.

Starting a Game

Use /lobby in any Discord channel to create a game lobby. Other players join by clicking the Join button. The host starts the game once everyone is in.

  • Free tier: up to 5 players, 3 games/day per player, 10 games/day per server
  • Games take around 20 minutes
  • Use /howtoplay inside Discord for a quick in-game guide

The Case is Revealed

When the game starts, the full case file is posted:

  • Crime Scene — the location, era, and atmosphere of the murder
  • Victim — who died and their background
  • 6 Suspects — each with a relation to the victim, a motive, a personality trait, their last known location, and their own alibi statement
  • 3 Opening Clues — the first pieces of evidence to begin your investigation
💡 Read every suspect profile before discussion begins. The details in their background and alibi statement matter more than they appear.

Discussion Phase

Each round opens with a timed discussion phase. Talk freely in the channel — share theories, challenge alibis, and cross-reference clues against suspect profiles. The timer runs whether you talk or not. Use it.

The key question each round: who can we confidently rule out? Not who is guilty — who is definitely innocent.

Voting Phase

After discussion, voting opens. Each player votes to clear one suspect they're confident is not the murderer. The suspect with a strict majority of votes is eliminated from suspicion.

  • No majority reached — no one is eliminated, the game continues
  • Innocent eliminated — they're cleared, the investigation continues
  • Murderer eliminated — the killer escapes. Game over immediately.
☠️ Critical rule: If your team votes out the actual murderer at any point, the game ends immediately and nobody wins. Be certain before you vote someone innocent.

A Case File button is visible during every voting round. Click it for a private digest of all suspects and every clue revealed so far — no scrolling up needed.

New Clue Each Round

After voting, a new piece of evidence is revealed. Clues get more precise as the game progresses — pay close attention to rounds 3 and 4. The later clues are designed to dismantle the misdirection, not reinforce it.

The Final Guess

After 4 rounds, every detective makes one final private guess — who is the murderer? Your answer is sealed until everyone has guessed or the timer expires.

Then the full truth is revealed one player at a time: each detective's verdict is unsealed, then the murderer's identity is announced along with their real motive, how they committed the crime, and — crucially — exactly how the Plottwyst was constructed to fool you.

Every detective who guessed correctly wins. Results are saved to /stats and /leaderboard automatically.

Commands

  • /lobby — Create a new game lobby
  • /howtoplay — In-game guide (ephemeral, only you see it)
  • /stats — Your personal detective record
  • /leaderboard — Top detectives in your server
  • /about — What is Plottwyst?
