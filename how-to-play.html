How to Play

1. Start a Game

A murder mystery case is generated with a victim, suspects, and hidden motives.

2. Study the Suspects

Each suspect has a personality, motive, and statement about the crime.

3. Discover Clues

New clues appear every round revealing new information about the suspects.

4. Discuss with Players

Debate with other players to determine who might be innocent.

5. Eliminate Suspects

Vote to eliminate suspects you believe are not the murderer.

6. Make the Final Guess

At the end of the game, each player guesses who the murderer is.
