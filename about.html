About PlotTwyst

PlotTwyst is a social deduction murder mystery game designed for Discord communities.

Each game generates a unique mystery with suspects, motives, clues and hidden twists. Players must analyze the evidence, debate suspects, and work together to uncover the killer.

The game combines elements from classic detective stories, party games, and social deduction mechanics.

Our goal is to create infinite replayable murder mysteries that challenge players’ logical reasoning and deduction skills.
