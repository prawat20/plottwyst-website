# Plottwyst — Murder Mystery for Discord

Plottwyst is a cooperative murder mystery game for Discord servers. It is free to play and requires no external apps or accounts — everything happens inside Discord using slash commands and interactive buttons.

## What Makes Plottwyst Different

Every case contains a **Plottwyst** — a deliberate misdirection woven into the evidence from the very first clue. The opening clues build a convincing case against the wrong suspect. The red herring has a motive, an opportunity, and seemingly damning evidence stacked against them. The real killer is hidden in plain sight. Players must see through the lie to win.

## How a Game Works

- A unique murder case is generated: victim background, 6 suspect profiles (each with a motive, personality trait, last known location, and alibi statement), and 3 opening clues
- Players discuss the evidence in a timed discussion phase each round
- Players vote to clear one suspect they believe is innocent — but vote out the actual murderer and the game ends immediately
- 4 rounds of evidence, each round revealing a new clue
- After round 4, every player makes one final private guess naming the murderer
- The full truth is revealed: the killer's identity, their real motive, and exactly how the Plottwyst was constructed

## Key Facts

- Platform: Discord (slash commands + interactive buttons)
- Players: 2–5 (free tier), 2–10 (premium)
- Game length: ~20 minutes
- Free tier: 3 games/day per player, 10 games/day per server
- Premium: unlimited games, more players, additional modes coming soon
- Every case is uniquely generated — no two investigations are ever the same
- Currently in open beta

## Genres Available

Victorian England, 1920s noir, modern corporate thriller, and more. Each genre has its own atmosphere and style of misdirection.

## Slash Commands

- `/lobby` — Create a new game lobby
- `/howtoplay` — In-game guide
- `/stats` — Personal detective record
- `/leaderboard` — Server rankings
- `/about` — What is Plottwyst?
- `/premium info` — Premium tier details
