Contact

Plottwyst is in open beta. Your feedback directly shapes what gets built next — bug reports, confusing clues, broken mechanics, ideas — all of it is valuable.

Get in Touch

📧 contact@plottwyst.app

For the fastest response on bugs or gameplay issues, email works best. Include the game channel, what happened, and roughly when — that helps track it down quickly.

What to Report

  • A clue that doesn't make sense or contradicts the solution
  • A suspect profile that feels inconsistent
  • A bot command that didn't respond or behaved unexpectedly
  • The Plottwyst misdirection that felt too obvious or too impossible
  • Anything that broke the immersion or confused your team
💡 You can also rate each case directly after the game using the feedback buttons — that feedback goes straight into the system and helps improve future cases automatically.

Partnerships & Press

Interested in featuring Plottwyst, partnering, or early access for a large community? Reach out at contact@plottwyst.app with the subject line Partnership.
